Shipping Policy

  • We deliver our products to all of UAE via Emirates Post.
  • Orders are processed within 1-3 business days, excluding weekends and holidays.
  • Daily cut-off: 5 PM Dubai Time. Orders after will ship the next business day.
  • Delivery fees shown at checkout. Free for orders 200+ AED.
  • Provide accurate address & contact details to avoid delays.
  • Cash on Delivery max: 3,000 AED.

Refund Policy

  • Returns accepted within 3 days of receipt. Items must be unused, in original condition & packaging.
  • After inspection, you will receive an email about refund approval. Refunds processed via original payment method in 5-10 days.
  • Mail your product to the Information Department or contact the call center.
